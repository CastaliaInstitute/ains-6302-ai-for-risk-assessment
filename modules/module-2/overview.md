# Module 2: Attack-Path Modeling and Breach Forecasting

## Overview

Models the enterprise as a graph and asks: given what an attacker can see, what is the likeliest path to crown-jewel assets, and how does risk evolve over time? Covers attack graphs, Bayesian networks, and time-series forecasting.

## Topics

- Attack graphs and shortest-path-to-impact analysis
- Bayesian networks for compromise propagation
- Time-series forecasting of breach likelihood
- Communicating uncertainty to non-technical stakeholders

## Module Deliverable

Construct an attack graph from a synthetic network map and identify the top-5 attack paths to a crown-jewel asset. Fit a Bayesian network to propagate compromise probabilities, and forecast next-quarter breach likelihood with uncertainty.
